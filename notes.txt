BSF stands for black soldier fly, an insects that its larvae has great economical value 
due to its proteinous nature to animals like poultry, fishes and even pigs.
These larvae acts on spoilt left over feeds at a very high rate, the more they act on it,
the faster they grow in size within a short period of time; mostly betweeen 14-21 days. 
Farmers that engage on BSF Farming make huge revenue from this endeavour because it doesnt 
take much capital to set up because spoilt foods are easily available at little or no cost, 
while the infrastructural set up are mostly locally sourced paint buckets, woods and nets.

The aim of this backend project is to provide a platform that would enable the Farmer
to easily manage and monitor the farming production activities that is been carried out
in the farm. This app will provide a seamless tracking of BSF farming activities for a
wide range of different batches. It will provide features such as batch initialization,
feeding activities, recommendations, harvesting and overall production analytics.
